CloakLM is a lightning-fast native macOS desktop app that automatically redacts highly sensitive PII from your PDFs locally before they ever touch the cloud LLMs.

Download for macOS Explore Features

Why Use CloakLM?

A suite of advanced privacy features designed for enterprise-grade data security.

🧠

Local AI Engine

We bundle IBM Docling and the cutting-edge GLiNER AI directly into the app. Your data is scanned and redacted 100% locally on your computer.

🔑

Bring Your Own Key

Plug in your own API keys for Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, or connect directly to local Ollama models completely for free.

📄

Intelligent PDF Parsing

Extracts deep layouts, tables, and complex formatting out of any PDF document seamlessly while maintaining context for the LLM.

🛡️

Absolute Privacy

Names, addresses, and organizations are swapped for `[PERSON_1]` or `[ORGANIZATION_1]`. The LLM's response is safely reconstituted back to the original entities on your end.

How It Works

Three simple steps to absolute data privacy.

1️⃣

Drop a PDF

Simply drag and drop any local document directly into the CloakLM window.

2️⃣

Local Redaction

Our secure on-device AI instantly scrubs the document of all sensitive PII offline.

3️⃣

Safe Analysis

The safely anonymized text is analyzed by your chosen LLM, ensuring data stays private.
